Who I am

Former Head of the ENT Unit at San Luca Hospital in Lucca (2001–Dec 2024). Specialized in head & neck oncologic surgery, endoscopic sinus surgery, maxillofacial, orbital and lacrimal duct surgery, and aesthetic medicine. More than 12,000 surgeries performed; lecturer and contract professor, speaker and author of over 160 scientific publications, organizer of 75 congresses, training courses and live surgeries.
